About Stepwell Housing.

Stepwell Housing provides safe, compliant and professionally managed accommodation solutions for local authorities, landlords and the communities they serve.

We work closely with property owners and local authorities to deliver high-quality housing that offers stability for residents, reliable returns for landlords and professional management throughout.

Our Mission

“To provide safe, well-maintained and professionally managed accommodation that supports residents, delivers value to landlords and contributes to stronger communities.”

Tenesh Deva — Co-Founder

Integrity

We act honestly and transparently in every relationship — with landlords, local authorities and the residents who call our properties home.

Quality

Every property is maintained to a high standard, with regular inspections and prompt repairs that keep homes safe and comfortable.

Partnership

We work alongside local authorities and property owners as long-term partners, built on trust and consistent, reliable service.

Responsibility

We meet our obligations on safety, compliance and energy standards, and hold ourselves accountable to the communities we serve.

At Stepwell Housing, we believe quality accommodation provides more than just a place to live. It creates stability, dignity and opportunity. We are committed to:

01 Safe, compliant homes
02 Responsive management
03 Resident wellbeing
04 Energy efficiency
05 Long-term partnerships

Supporting Communities.

Good housing helps create stronger communities. When homes are safe, well managed and properly maintained, residents are more settled, landlords see consistent returns and local authorities can rely on a stable supply of quality accommodation. Our goal is simple: to provide safe, compliant and professionally managed accommodation that benefits people, properties and communities.

Who We Are

Founded in Croydon, Stepwell Housing was built on the belief that property management should be straightforward, reliable and genuinely stress-free for landlords. We work directly with local authorities and housing bodies to provide quality accommodation for individuals and families.

Our team brings together expertise in property management, compliance, maintenance and resident welfare — all under one roof. Every landlord who works with us benefits from a dedicated point of contact, not a call centre.

What We Do

We specialise in three core services: guaranteed rent schemes, fully managed property services and in-house maintenance and repairs. Together, these create a complete, hands-off solution for landlords who want consistent income without the hassle of day-to-day management.

Whether you own a single house or a portfolio of properties, our approach is the same — protect your asset, support your residents and pay you on time, every time.

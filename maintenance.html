Our Property Care Process

From the moment an issue is reported to the moment it's resolved, every step is managed, tracked and communicated clearly.

Step 01
Issue Reported
Resident or landlord flags the issue via our dedicated reporting line or portal.
Step 02
Issue Logged & Assessed
Our team logs the issue, assesses urgency and assigns the right professional.
Step 03
Contractor Dispatched
A qualified in-house engineer or specialist contractor is sent promptly.
Step 04
Work Carried Out
The repair or maintenance task is completed to a high standard on-site.
Step 05
Quality Checked
Work is verified and signed off before the job is marked complete.
Step 06
Issue Resolved
Landlord is notified with a full update. Resident is satisfied. Property protected.

What We Cover

General Repairs

Doors, windows, locks, flooring, walls and general wear-and-tear fixes handled quickly and professionally.

Plumbing

Electrical

Fuse board issues, lighting faults, socket repairs and electrical safety checks carried out by qualified engineers.

Heating & Boilers

Communal Areas
